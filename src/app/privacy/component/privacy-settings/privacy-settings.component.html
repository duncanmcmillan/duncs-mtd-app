Privacy & Data

What data is collected

  • Your HMRC OAuth access and refresh tokens (stored encrypted on this device)
  • Your HMRC client ID and client secret (stored encrypted on this device)
  • No personal data is sent to any server other than HMRC's own APIs over TLS

Lawful basis

Processing is carried out under Article 6(1)(b) UK GDPR — performance of a contract. The app processes credentials solely to submit Making Tax Digital returns on your behalf as required by HMRC.

Data retention

Tokens are cleared automatically when you sign out. Config credentials remain until you explicitly clear them or delete all data below.

Your rights

You have the right to erasure under Article 17 UK GDPR. Use the button below to delete all data held on this device. This action cannot be undone.

ICO registration

If you process clients' personal data commercially you may need to register with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO). Visit ico.org.uk/registration for guidance.
