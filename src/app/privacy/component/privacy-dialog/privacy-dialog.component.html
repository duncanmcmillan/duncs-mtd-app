Privacy Notice

Before you use this application, please read how it handles your data.

What data is collected

  • Your HMRC OAuth access and refresh tokens (stored encrypted on this device)
  • Your HMRC client ID and client secret (stored encrypted on this device)
  • No personal data is sent to any server other than HMRC's own APIs over TLS

Lawful basis

Processing is carried out under Article 6(1)(b) UK GDPR — performance of a contract. The app processes credentials solely to submit Making Tax Digital returns on your behalf as required by HMRC.

Data retention & deletion

Tokens are cleared when you sign out. You can delete all stored data at any time via Settings → Privacy → Delete All My Data.

ICO registration

If you use this application to process clients' personal data commercially you may need to register with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO). Visit ico.org.uk/registration for guidance.
